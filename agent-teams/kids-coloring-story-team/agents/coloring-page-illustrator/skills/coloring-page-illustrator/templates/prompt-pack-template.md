## Global Prompt Constraints

- Cute black-and-white printable coloring page line art matching children's coloring bookmarks and Bible verse coloring sheets.
- Simple doodle-style outlines, rounded shapes, closed colorable areas, friendly calm expressions, child-safe scene, generous white space.
- One full A4 page image for one story beat only. Do not create a collage, grid, contact sheet, comic strip, thumbnail board, or multi-panel layout.
- Leave safe margins for printing.
- Avoid copyrighted characters or brand-like designs.
- Avoid realistic Bible illustration, dramatic action, scary faces, comic-book battle style, manga style, adult coloring-book detail, gray shading, dense hatching, and complex backgrounds.
